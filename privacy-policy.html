Privacy Policy

A simple template privacy policy for a small business website.

Template notice: This privacy policy is provided as a general starting point for the website owner to review, customize, and approve. It is not legal advice.

Probeck Land Surveyors respects your privacy. This website collects only the information you choose to submit through the contact form.

Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, property address, survey type, and message. This information is provided voluntarily so we can respond to your inquiry.

How We Use Information

We use contact form information solely to respond to questions, provide requested information, and communicate about potential surveying services. We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Contact Form Processing

Form submissions are processed through Web3Forms, a third-party form service that forwards the information to the business email address selected by the website owner. The owner should review Web3Forms' current terms and privacy practices before launch.

Data Sharing

We may share information only when necessary to respond to your request, comply with legal obligations, or protect the rights and safety of the business and its clients.

Data Retention

Inquiry information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request and maintain business records.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request that we update or delete information you previously submitted, subject to any legal or business recordkeeping requirements.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, use the contact form on the Contact page.
